Research Direction

We aim to build reliable and adaptive AI systems that quantify uncertainty in new data and use it to quickly adapt to changing environments. Our research focuses on:

Continual and Adaptive Learning

Learning new dataset quickly and efficiently by leveraging previously learned knowledge.

link1 link2

Uncertainty-Aware Learning

Quantifying uncertainty to represent what AI models know and don't know.

link1 link2

Multimodal Foundation Models

Developing foundation models for learning and inference across multiple modalities, such as text, images, audio, and sensor data.

link1 link2

Scientific AI for Physical Systems

Developing AI methods that incorporate physical laws and differential equations to model, simulate, and predict real-world physical systems.
